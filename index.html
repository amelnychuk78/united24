Fine Dine Auction Reception

We are pleased to invite you to join us for a unique charity event, organized by The Embassy of Ukraine, with the support of President Zelenskyy.

Ukraine is fighting not only for its own freedom but for the freedom of the entire democratic world. Only together do we have the potential to stop the war Russia has started. To rebuild what Russia has destroyed.

UNITED24 was launched by the President of Ukraine, Volodymyr Zelenskyy, to unite people worldwide in their desire to support Ukraine.

The first element of UNITED24 is the fundraising platform that enables making a one-click donation from any country and receiving a report on how funds were allocated. The main goal is to increase the number of donations for Ukraine and to ensure the efficiency and transparency of their distribution.

We're hosting this event to present our partners and special friends of Ukraine with opportunities for cooperation with UNITED24 and raise funds during the charity auction with unique items from the President of Ukraine, Kalush Orchestra, and others to protect, save and rebuild Ukraine.

Your presence will give a special meaning to this event.
We will never forget your contribution to the victory of Ukraine. To the victory of freedom.
